How This System Works

Every email above was generated by an 8-agent AI pipeline that enriches, personalizes, and quality-checks each prospect touch. Here's the process:

1

Prospect Discovery

Identify SMB property managers (10-150 units) across target vacation rental markets.

2

Data Enrichment

Detect PMS platform, portfolio size, market seasonality, and direct booking presence via domain intel.

3

Personalization Engine

AI generates a unique opener, subject line, and value proposition tailored to each prospect's specific situation.

4

Quality Gate

Each email is scored on relevance, tone, and accuracy. Only emails scoring 8+ are included in the send queue.

5

Delivery & Tracking

Emails are drafted, scheduled, and tracked. Hot replies are auto-classified and escalated for human follow-up.
