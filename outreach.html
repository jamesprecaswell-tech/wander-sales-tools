How This Pipeline Was Built

Every email in this portfolio came from a structured 5-stage AI pipeline. Prospect data is enriched, verified, and scored before a single word is written. Here is the process:

1

Prospect Discovery

Identify SMB property managers (10-150 units) across target vacation rental markets using listing data and operator directories.

2

Data Enrichment

Detect PMS platform, portfolio count, market ADR, seasonality curve, and direct booking presence via domain and listing intel.

3

Personalization Engine

AI drafts a unique opener, subject line, and revenue calculation specific to each prospect's PMS, market, and unit count.

4

Quality Gate

Each email is scored on relevance, tone, and factual accuracy. Only emails scoring 8 or above move to the send queue.

5

Delivery and Tracking

Emails are scheduled and tracked. Hot replies are auto-classified and escalated for same-day human follow-up.
